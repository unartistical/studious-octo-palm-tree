Some random information about coffee

Coffee beans are only called 'beans' because of the resemblance - they are actually berries.

Studies have shown that drinking coffee may lower your risk of depression.

Thanks to it being jam-packed with antioxidants, coffee remains one of the healthiest beverages in the world.

Bees are the main pollinators of coffee plants. The flower also contains caffeinated nectar.
